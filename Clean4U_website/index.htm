About Clean 4 U

TClean 4 U Ltd. was established in 2016 with the aim of offering quality security and professional cleaning services on the market, applying an innovative approach and using a sustainable model that underpins the high-tech capabilities of the evolving environment in combination with knowledge and skills of specialists in the field of soft services. Behind our confidence that we are different lies the motivation of the management and employees of the company - proven professionals with long professional experience in the field of security and cleaning services for businesses in Bulgarian and international companies. The constant search and implementation of technological solutions facilitates both our work and our clients' access to the essence of the services offered.

Cleaning windows

In the big city, soiled windows are common. Initially, washing them seems like an easy and unpretentious task, but as with all services, there are details that only professionals can accomplish. We can wash your double-sided or double-sided windows, clean the residue of stickers, foils, various types of branding, dirt, limestone and more. In order to reach even the most inaccessible parts, we have special equipment, preparations and trained specialists.

Floor cleaning

The service can be performed at any time of the year, regardless of the season. Washing of upholstered floors and furniture is done through professional cleaning products and extractors that guarantee the quality and protection of fabrics and fabrics. The activity begins with vacuuming to remove dusty and dirty areas and then proceeds to washing. Contamination caused by spilled liquids, other stains and traces of pets can be eliminated.

Facade cleaning

The face of the building or the entrance to the premises are some of the most important parts of a person's first impression. Facade cleaning is a specific activity that requires a preliminary inspection, since the required time, preparations and equipment are determined by the materials of the cladding, the location and the volume of work. The work is carried out by qualified employees with the appropriate certificates and in compliance with technological safety requirements.
